vietnam

VIETNAM was really out of my comfort zone. Too many motorbikes, people, noise, food, smels.. But on the other hand I must say vietnamese people were super nice and helpful. And ofcourse, we also enjoyed their beutiful nature.

We went to northern Vietnam in May 2019 and traveled for almost three weeks there. We started with a bus ride from Prague to Munich with RegioJet, becase we had a cheap flight tickets from Munich to Bejing, China, and after 15 hours transfer from there to Hanoi.

We decided to spend one night in Munich and enjoy the city a little as well. Our flight was the next day in the afternoon, so we had plenty of time for relax and exploring.

Then we flew ten hours from Munich to Bejing, where planed to go out from the airport to see the city, but we were so exhosted after the flight and had huge jetlag, therefore decided to take an offer from air China and get transfer hotel. We slept for around eight hours there.

The next day in the morning we flew from Bejing to Hanoi. It was three hours flight. When we arrived to the city center, it was crazy.
