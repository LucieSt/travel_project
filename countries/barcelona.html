BARCELONA

I love this city, bicycle-friendly, every part is different, has great architecture, you can go to the beach by subway...


We went there for the first time together in november 2016 for three days, then spent seven weeks there during the summer 2017 and we came back for a few days this july (2019), on my birthday.

First night in Norway we slept somewhere near Oslo in our tents (in Norway it is allowed to build a tent or sleep anywhere, but at least 50m from private property). It started to rain in the evening and was raining all night, so lot of our stuff was wet when we got up and we didn't know what to do next. We decided to rest a little and booked one or two nights at some hostel near Lillehammer. There we dried our tents and clothes.

Then we made a shotr trip to Lillehammer Olympic Park.
